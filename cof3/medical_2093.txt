This is a case study involving a worker's compensation claim. It is important to understand the legal context in which it occurs so that you can identify possible conflicts. The worker is experiencing pain. The worker is depressed, which makes it hard for him to work. The two people need to understand the legal process. It is important for them to ask questions about the legal process in order to help the worker. It is also important for them to understand that the worker can't return to work until the medical assessments are complete.
