This passage shows a couple in a disagreement. This passage is a good example of a conflict between a couple. The intent here is to increase understanding of conflict resolution. Conflict resolution is a very important skill to have, especially for couples. Conflict resolution skills can be learned. This couple seems to have a very different perspective on the situation. In order to resolve this conflict, they need to find a common perspective. In this case, the wife seems to be the initiator of the conflict. She is at a point where she needs to understand her perspective and needs to understand her feelings. She also needs to understand the feelings of her husband. She needs to understand how her feelings and perspective are different from his, and why she wants different things from him.
