Economics is not the most interesting subject for most people. However, this passage has something exciting to offer. It offers a solution to a common problem. It shows the reader how to be happier. The solution is to spend money! This is a great learning opportunity. There are multiple things to learn here. First of all, the economics concepts of supply and demand come into play. People need to understand how supply and demand work. Secondly, people need to understand what goes into the decisions they make about money. It is helpful to read the sidebar “The Psychology of Money” in order to understand what is happening in the mind of the consumer. This is the psychology of the consumer that is relevant here.
